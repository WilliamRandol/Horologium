    Horologium

    Horologium is a roman numeral digital clock. It was created using html5, css, and javascript. The author of it is William Randol, with RSPCT.us.

    Please use one of the social media buttons at the top to share this with your friends. and feel free to check the ads at the bottom of the screen.

    These adds will automatically show for thirty seconds every two minutes.
